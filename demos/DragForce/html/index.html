Directions

Choose objects for simulation through the dropdown menu. For custom object, select "custom" and then enter the physical properties.

Press "Start" button to start the animation.

Press "Stop" to interrupt the animation. Beware that the animation can't be resumed once interrupted.

Details

On calculation

The equation for drag force calculation is: $$F = \frac{C_D \rho V^2 A}{2}$$ Where \( C_D \) is the drag coefficient. \( \rho \) is the desity of surrounding fluid, which is air in our case. \( A \) is the cross-sectional area of the object.

On object

The parachutist weights 174 kg with parachute. The parachute has a diameter of 35 feet (11m).
