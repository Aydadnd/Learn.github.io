Directions

This simulation calculates and generates a vector field that visualizes the direction of moving fluid particals around a 1 meter sphere. One of three fluids can be selected from the dropdown menu. The velocity of selected fluid can be adjusted with the slider bar. The vector field will automaticaly update if there are changes.
